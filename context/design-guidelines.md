# Reel Records — Design Guidelines

> **Status: awaiting the Claude Design wireframe.** Fill this in from the mockup once shared.
> This is the reference for the app's look, feel, and interaction language.

## Design intent

**Premium fishing journal × modern social tracking app.** Tactile, image-forward, personal, and
calm — a record you're proud to keep, not a noisy feed.

## To capture from the wireframe

- **Visual language:** color palette, typography, iconography, imagery treatment.
- **Layout:** key screens and their hierarchy (log entry, catch detail, history/gallery, stats).
- **Interaction:** navigation model, logging flow, gestures, empty/loading states.
- **Tone:** copy voice, labels, microcopy.
- **Assets:** where design files / exports live.

## Notes

- Optimize the logging flow for one-handed, in-the-field, possibly-offline use.
